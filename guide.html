Starkle

Your family's star-powered planner

Kids do their tasks, earn stars, and trade them for rewards.

🌟What is Starkle?

  • A friendly planner for the whole family.
  • Everyone gets their own profile.
  • Finish tasks and routines to earn ⭐ stars.
  • Save up stars and swap them for rewards you choose.

📲Put it on your phone

  • Open the link in your browser (Chrome works best).
  • Tap the browser menu, then “Add to Home screen”.
  • It opens like a real app — and works without internet.

🧭The tabs, at a glance

  • 📅Calendar — where the app opens; events, birthdays and anniversaries.
  • ✅Tasks — tick off jobs to earn stars.
  • ⭐Rewards — treats to “buy” with stars.
  • 📝Lists — shared lists like shopping or to-dos.
  • ⚙️Settings — people, backup and sign-in.
  • 🔒Lock — the parent lock (more below).

⭐Stars & rewards

  • Tap a task or routine to mark it done — stars are added for you.
  • Set up rewards with a star price, e.g. “Movie night = 20 ⭐”.
  • When someone has saved enough, they redeem the reward.
  • Set family goals with a progress bar everyone can watch fill up.

🔒Parent lock

  • Set a PIN so kids can't change stars or settings.
  • Tap the lock to switch between parent and kid mode.

☁️Syncing & sharing (optional)

  • Works fully without signing in — data stays on your phone.
  • Want it on your phone and tablet? Sign in with Google to sync them.
Good to know: each Google account has its own separate planner. Signing in on a new account starts a fresh one — it doesn't show someone else's data.

💾Keep your data safe

  • Settings → Export backup saves a copy as a file.
  • Import backup brings it back, or moves it to a new phone.
